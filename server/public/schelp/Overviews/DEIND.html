        DEIND
        Extension

        Ugen collection developed within the DEIND project
        See also: JPverb, Greyhole, ComplexRes, DiodeRingMod, DNoiseRing, RMS

        Description

        UGens

        JPverb
        Algorithmic reverberator, designed to produce long and rich reverberant tails
        Greyhole
        A complex echo-like effect
        ComplexRes
        FM-modulable resonating filter
        DiodeRingMod
        Ring modulation based on the physical model of a diode
        DNoiseRing
        Demand rate implementation of a Wiard noise ring
        RMS
        calculate the root mean square of a low-pass filtered signal (added 2016)

        Information

        UGens in this package were originally developed within the DEIND project in 2013.

        The project aimed to connect people with autistic spectrum disorder with the field of contemporary electronic and digital music practice. People with severe autistic spectrum disorders were invited to take part in the design process of electronic instruments. To facilitate music practice, we aimed for a holistic instrument experience rather than a modular approach in which the underlying modules of electronic instruments – interface/mapping/sound synthesis – would become too evident and possibly confuse the player too much.

        The close integration of target group members encouraged a bilateral learning process for both researchers and participants.

        During the project we designed and implemented unit generators to support the development of DEIND instruments.

        More information at http://tai-studio.org/portfolio/deind.html .
